Saygo

I'm a Polish artist, I enjoy software and Linux. Computers are an inspiration for my artworks as well as my medium of choice.

Art

I love the unique qualities of traditional art - how watercolors granulate, how paint strokes look, and how paper feels. But for some reason, we don't appreciate digital art's unique qualities in the same way. Instead, people often dismiss digital effects as cheap or fake. I think that's wrong, which is why I proudly include digital elements in my work. They're just as worthy of appreciation. See my works here.

Software

I use NixOS and create artwork using Krita, Inkscape, Blender and various code libraries. Click here to see my desktop, dotfiles are available on my github profile.
